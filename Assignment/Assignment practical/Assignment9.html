New York City

: New York City, often called NYC, is the most populous city in the United States, located in southeastern New York State at the mouth of the Hudson River. It's a global hub for culture, finance, media, and more. The city is comprised of five boroughs: Manhattan, Brooklyn, The Bronx, Queens, and Staten Island.

Boroughs: NYC is comprised of five boroughs: Manhattan, Brooklyn, The Bronx, Queens, and Staten Island.

Population: The city itself has a population of over 8 million, with the metropolitan area exceeding 22 million. Iconic landmarks include the Statue of Liberty, the Empire State Building, and Central Park.

History: NYC's history dates back to a Dutch trading post established around 1624, which later became New Amsterdam and eventually New York.
