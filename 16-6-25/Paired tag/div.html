Lord Krishna

Krishna is a major deity in Hinduism, worshipped as the eighth avatar of Vishnu and also as the Supreme God in his own right. He is revered as the god of protection, compassion, tenderness, and love. His birthday, Krishna Janmashtami, is a major Hindu festival.
