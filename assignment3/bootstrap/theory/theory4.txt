3. List and explain at least three Bootstrap components.

i) Navbar

A ready-made menu bar at the top of the website.
Works on both desktop and mobile (collapses into a button on small screens).

ii) Cards

A small box that can contain an image, title, text, and button.
Used for portfolios, products, or blogs.

iii) Buttons

Pre-designed buttons with different colors and sizes.
Example: .btn-primary (blue button), .btn-success (green button).
