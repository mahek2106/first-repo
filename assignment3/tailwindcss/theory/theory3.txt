Five Common Tailwind Classes

i)text-center

Aligns text to the center.

<p class="text-center">This text is centered</p>


ii)bg-blue-500

Gives a blue background color (shade 500).

<div class="bg-blue-500 text-white p-4">Blue Box</div>


iii)p-4

Adds padding (space inside) all around the element.

<div class="p-4 border">Box with padding</div>


iv)rounded-lg

Makes corners rounded (like a card or button).

<button class="bg-green-500 text-white rounded-lg px-4 py-2">Click Me</button>


v)shadow-md

Adds a medium shadow, making the element look lifted.

<div class="shadow-md p-4">Box with shadow</div>
